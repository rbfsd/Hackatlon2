About Us


Welcome to DT&M, a place of remembrance and honor. At DT&M, we are committed to preserving the memory of our heroes who selflessly gave their lives in defense of our homeland.

Following the 7th October 2023, our organization is driven by a deep sense of gratitude and a solemn duty to ensure that the sacrifices of our heroes are never forgotten. We believe in the power of remembrance to unite communities and inspire future generations.


Our Mission:

  • Honoring the Sacrifices: We strive to honor and commemorate the sacrifices made by our heroes, recognizing their bravery and dedication.

  • Community Engagement: Fostering a sense of community and solidarity through events, remembrance ceremonies, and educational initiatives.

  • Preserving History: Working to preserve the historical significance of the events and individuals who shaped our nation's history.


Join us in our mission to ensure that the stories of our heroes live on and continue to inspire generations to come.
